Colophon

The Duel
was published in 1891 by
Anton Chekhov.
It was translated from Russian between 1916 and 1922 by
Constance Garnett.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on transcriptions produced between 2004 and 2006 by
James Rusk
for
Project Gutenberg.

The cover page is adapted from
Eugene Onegin and Vladimir Lensky’s Duel,
a painting completed in 1899 by
Ilya Repin.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
